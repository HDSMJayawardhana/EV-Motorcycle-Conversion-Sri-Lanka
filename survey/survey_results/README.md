# 📊 Market Survey — EV Motorcycle Conversion in Sri Lanka

A comprehensive field survey conducted to understand motorcycle rider preferences,
fuel consumption patterns, and willingness to adopt EV conversion technology in Sri Lanka.

---

## 📋 Survey Details

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| Survey Method | Google Forms (Online) + Direct Interviews |
| Total Respondents | 100+ motorcycle riders |
| Survey Period | Semester 07 (2024) |
| Districts Covered | Kandy, Gampaha, Colombo, Kurunegala |
| Target Group | Daily motorcycle commuters |

---

## 🏍️ Sri Lanka Motorcycle Market Context

### Motorcycle Dominance in Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka has one of the highest motorcycle-to-population ratios in South Asia.
Motorcycles are the primary mode of transport for millions of Sri Lankans.

| Year | Registered Motorcycles | % of Total Vehicles |
|------|------------------------|---------------------|
| 2019 | 3,892,000 | 62.4% |
| 2020 | 4,100,000 | 63.1% |
| 2021 | 4,350,000 | 64.2% |
| 2022 | 4,580,000 | 65.0% |
| 2023 | 4,780,000 | 65.8% |

> Source: Department of Motor Traffic (DMT), Sri Lanka
